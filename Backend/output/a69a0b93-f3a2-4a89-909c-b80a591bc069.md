## Introduction

## Key Concepts

* A clock has two hands: the hour hand (H.H.) and the minute hand (M.H.).
* The hour hand moves 30° in one hour and 6° in one minute.
* The minute hand moves 6° in one minute.
* The angle between the hour and minute hands can be calculated using the formula:
\[ \text{Angle} = 30H - \frac{11}{2}M \]
where H is the hour and M is the minute.
